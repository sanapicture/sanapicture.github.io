Minatozaki Sana

TWICE Girl Group

Minatozaki Sana ( Japanese: 湊 崎 紗 夏 ). Born in Osaka , Japan , December 29, 1996, also known as Sana ( Hangul: 사나 ), is a Japanese singer. She is a member of the international K-pop music group TWICE , formed by JYP Entertainment through the reality show SIXTEEN in 2015.


Sana interest in K-Pop was influenced by the popularity of SNSD and KARA that climbed in Japan at the time. In her final year in junior high school, she joined JYP Entertainment through an audition on April 13, 2012. While still a trainee , Sana has appeared on the GOT7 music video titled "A" and Junho's music video entitled " Feel ". Sana follows the SIXTEEN reality show held by JYP Entertainment to form a new girl group. In the final episode of SIXTEEN , Sana is declared elected as a Twice member. When TWICE comeback and release music for their latest song "CHEER UP" on April 25, 2016, Sana Shy Shy Shy becomes viral and gaining popularity in all circles, both among fans and other korean idols.
